Note callouts, alert states, architecture-stack status indicators. Apply to one edge only.
Stroke widths above 2 pt feel heavy and start to look decorative. If a frame needs more emphasis, use color (the green border on unit cards) or the accent stripe — not a thicker line.
Roundness — proposed. Corner radius is variable: 0 pt for technical diagrams (architecture, topology) where right angles signal precision, and up to 4 pt for cards and notes. The exact ceiling isn't locked yet — flag a preference. Pick one value per composition; don't mix 0 and 4 in the same view.

Creative options

The Academy frame is simple by design — a clean line. Keep it that way.

Nesting pattern for grouping. Section is gray-050 with no border; cards inside are white with 1 pt border.

Don't

Don't
Dashed border
Reserved for diagram annotation only — never on content frames.
Don't
Double lines
Decorative. Doesn't fit the Academy's flat aesthetic.
Don't
Drop shadow
No shadows on any frame. Surfaces are flat.
Don't
Gradient fill
Solid colors only. See Color > Gradients.
Don't
Pill / extreme rounding
Stay at 0, 4, or 8 pt corner radius. Pills are for badges only.
Don't
Decorative fills or patterns
No textures, stripes, dots, or other surface decoration.
