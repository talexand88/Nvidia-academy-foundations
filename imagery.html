Imagery

Three families. One brand voice. No stock smiles.

Three Families — Pick By Intent

Photography
Real hardware, data centers, GPUs, labs, real people learning. Use when the topic is concrete and the image adds evidence.
Illustration
Concept diagrams, monoline line art matching the icon library. Use when the idea is abstract or the photo would be too literal.
3D hero composition
Course title cards, video openers, web hero. Sourced from NVDAM — see Logos.

Photography Style

Data centers · racks · server halls
Hardware · GPUs · boards · NVIDIA-Certified Systems
Labs · learners · workstations
Subjects are real NVIDIA hardware, data center environments, and learning contexts. Get photography from the corporate brand team or NVDAM — don't pull from generic stock libraries.

Photo Treatment

Crop generously
Tight crops on the subject. Avoid wide-angle establishing shots that lose the focal point.
Dark tonality
Photos lean dark — server halls, server-room lighting, deep contrast. Matches the brand surface.
Cool color cast
Blue and green ambient light dominates. Avoid warm orange casts unless documenting a specific environment.
No filters
Black-and-white, sepia, vintage, lens-blur filters all off. The photo is what it is.

Illustration Style

Illustrations match the monoline icon style — same stroke weight (1.5 pt nominal), same green or black, same 48-unit grid. They are essentially icons drawn larger. Don't create illustrations in a different style; if you need an illustration, request an icon-style asset.

Don'ts

Don't
Generic stock photography
No smiling office workers, handshakes, lightbulbs, or "futuristic AI brain" cliches. They cheapen the course.
Don't
Heavy color overlays
Don't tint photos green, purple, or any brand color. The photo's own tonality is the treatment.
Don't
Big text overlaid on photos
Photos and titles each need their own space. Put the title in the lower-left clear zone, not floating over the subject.
Don't
Cartoon / mascot illustrations
Illustrations are monoline matching the icon style. No characters, mascots, or playful cartoon styles.
×
Don't
Low resolution images
Photos appear at slide size (1920+ px). Web hero images at 2× the rendered width. Anything pixelated breaks the brand.
Don't
Photos on brand-color backgrounds
Photos sit on neutral surfaces — black, white, gray. Don't place them on NVIDIA Green or section colors.
