Clear space around the logo

NVIDIA
Minimum clear space on every side = the height of the N in the NVIDIA wordmark.
The rule is proportional, not fixed: clear space scales with the logo. Whatever pt or px size the wordmark renders at, the clear space on all four sides equals that same height. The bigger the logo, the more breathing room around it.

3D hero composition

The rendered 3D NVIDIA "eye" you see on course title cards is not a logo — it's a sanctioned brand illustration. You don't generate it; you grab the approved asset from NVDAM.
Get the asset from NVDAM. Don't generate, render, or modify hero compositions yourself. If a needed variant doesn't exist, request it through the corporate brand team.
